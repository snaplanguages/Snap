Explanation: The question asks for the distance to the beach (meters from beach). The man confirms that the apartments are not far from the beach, specifically "only 300 metres". The number to enter is 300.

"Something to do with sun... Sunshine, is it? / I think you meant the Sunshade Apartments."
Explanation: The woman forgets the exact name of the apartment (guessing Sunshine), but the man corrects her, stating the name is Sunshade. This correctly precedes the word "Apartments" in the table.

Explanation: The prompt says "Greek paintings and...". In the audio, the man describes The Grand apartments as having Greek paintings in every room and a balcony outside.
